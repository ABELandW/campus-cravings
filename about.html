About Campus Cravings

Campus Cravings is an independent food review platform created by 2 students at Victoria University, Melbourne. Our goal is to make dining decisions easier for everyone — regardless of their background, budget, or dietary preferences. We believe great food should be accessible to all people.

Our reviews are written honestly and without payment from restaurants. Every visit is made independently and every score is based on the same five-point criteria: taste, presentation, value, service, and atmosphere.

♿ Accessibility Features

Campus Cravings is built to be inclusive and accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

1. 🔊 Read Aloud (Text-to-Speech)

Highlight any text on this page and click the button below to have it read aloud.

2. 🔤 Adjustable Font Size

Use the buttons below to increase or decrease the text size on this page.

3. 🎨 High Contrast Mode

Toggle high contrast mode for users with visual impairments or colour-blindness.

4. ⌨️ Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements on this website — buttons, links, and form fields — can be accessed using the Tab key. Press Enter to activate buttons. This ensures all users can navigate without a mouse.

🤝 Inclusive Language Commitment

Campus Cravings is committed to using inclusive and respectful language across all content on this website. We use gender-neutral terms such as "visitors," "users," and "people" and avoid assumptions about gender, cultural background, or dietary beliefs.

We celebrate food from all cultures and treat every cuisine with equal respect. Our reviews aim to introduce people to diverse culinary traditions rather than rank them against one another.

If you notice any language in our content that feels disrespectful or exclusionary, please contact us and we will address it promptly.
