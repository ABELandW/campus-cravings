How We Rate Restaurants

Every review is scored across five categories, each equally weighted:

  1. Taste – Is the food delicious and well-seasoned?
  2. Presentation – Does it look as good as it tastes?
  3. Value for Money – Is the price fair for the portion?
  4. Service – Were the staff friendly and efficient?
  5. Atmosphere – Is the venue comfortable and welcoming?
